BMI & Wellness Planner

A free, evidence-based tool for calculating BMI and generating personalized health plans.

About This Tool

This open-source Python utility helps you:

  • Calculate your BMI using the WHO-standard formula
  • Determine your weight category (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, Obese)
  • Receive a personalized diet, exercise, and lifestyle plan based on your age and BMI
  • Follow recommendations aligned with guidelines from WHO, CDC, and Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health

How to Use

  1. Download the script: bmi_wellness_planner.py
  2. Open your terminal and run:
    python bmi_wellness_planner.py
  3. Follow the prompts to enter your age, height, and weight (metric or imperial supported).
  4. Receive your BMI and a science-backed wellness plan instantly!
